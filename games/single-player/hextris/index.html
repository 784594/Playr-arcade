How To Play

  • Rotate left with A or Left Arrow.
  • Rotate right with D or Right Arrow.
  • Hold S or Down Arrow to make incoming blocks fall faster.
  • Match 3 or more connected top blocks of the same colour around the ring to clear them.
  • If any lane stack fills all available slots, the run ends.

Clear Rules

Connected same-colour arcs clear together. Chain clears give bigger score bursts. Survival time is the main leaderboard metric.
