How It Plays

The feel stays faithful: inertia matters, shots are limited, rocks split, and the field wraps forever.

Rules

  • Large asteroids split into medium and small fragments.
  • Clearing a wave spawns a denser new field.
  • Hit the ship and you lose a life after a short grace period.
  • Hyperspace is safe-ish, not safe.

Controls

  • Rotate: Arrow Left / Arrow Right or A / D
  • Thrust: Arrow Up or W
  • Fire: Space or Enter
  • Pause: P or Escape
  • Hyperspace: Shift or Mobile button
